About Our Company

Serving Spokane Valley homeowners with quality craftsmanship since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Residential Home Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide Spokane Valley homeowners with high-quality, reliable home improvement services from a local company they could trust. What started as a small family-operated business has grown into one of the region's most respected home improvement contractors.

As lifelong residents of Spokane Valley, we understand the unique challenges our climate presents for homes. From harsh winters to sunny summers, we've experienced it all firsthand. This local knowledge drives our commitment to installing products that are specifically designed to withstand Pacific Northwest weather conditions.

Our journey has been guided by core values of integrity, craftsmanship, and community commitment. We believe that when you improve a home, you're not just installing windows, doors, or siding—you're enhancing someone's sanctuary and investing in their family's comfort and security.
