Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical installation take?

Most window replacements take 1-2 days, door installations 1 day, and siding projects 1-2 weeks depending on home size. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options through our trusted partners. We can discuss these options during your free consultation.

Are your products covered by warranty?

All our products come with manufacturer warranties (up to lifetime on some products), and our installation work is backed by our own warranty for your peace of mind.

Do I need to be home during installation?

For most installations, you don't need to be present once we've discussed project details. However, we recommend being available for the initial and final walkthrough.
