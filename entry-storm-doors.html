ProVia Door Technology: The Difference is in the Details

What sets ProVia apart? A relentless focus on every component, down to the smallest detail. From material selection to hardware engineering, each element is designed to withstand heat, cold, and daily use while keeping your family comfortable and reducing energy bills.

Door Slab Construction

Polyurethane foam core for R-values up to R-17. Superior insulation, sound reduction, and dent resistance.

Composite Frames

Engineered for a square, true fit that won't rot or warp. Precision milling ensures seamless weatherstripping contact.

Triple Weatherstripping

Flexible vinyl bulb and compression seals at the jamb, head, and threshold. Virtually eliminates air and water infiltration.

Advanced Hardware

Heavy-duty hinges, multi-point lock systems, and reinforced strike plates. Tested for thousands of open/close cycles.

Thermal Break

Non-conductive material between interior and exterior metal surfaces. Stops cold transfer and prevents condensation.

Enduring Finishes

Factory-applied, baked-on coatings. Resist UV rays, moisture, and temperature swings. Backed by long-term warranties.
